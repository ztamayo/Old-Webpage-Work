Fiesta Hermosa
Visit the Fiesta Hermosa site.

  1. When does the festival take place?

  2. Who do I contact for more information?

  3. Where do I park?





















  1. The festival takes place every year on Memorial Day and Labor Day Weekend.

  2. For further information, contact Hermosa Beach Chamber at (310) 376-0951, or or Mike Bell of Bell Event Services at (310) 488-9457.

  3. Free parking is available at Northrop Grumman Building O4 at 15092 Aviation Blvd. A free shuttle service runs from 7:30 a.m. to 7:30 p.m. each day of Fiesta Hermosa.
