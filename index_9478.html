My Favorite Video Games

Introduction


In this website I will describe my recent favorite video games. All the games were played on a Playstation 3 system. The games listed on this site were all played by me, and they were played in the last six months.

A page is dedicated to each game. Each page will contain a description of the game, images of the game and my comments on the game. The games I will discuss are:
